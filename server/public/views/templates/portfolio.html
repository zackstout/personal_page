Solo Project

World Weaver

  • Design worlds (composed of a rotatable cannon, a target, obstacles, and a set of portals) for other users to play through and solve.
  • Save your worlds mid-edit, update the sizes and positions of your obstacles, and keep track of your favorite worlds as well as the worlds you've created.
  • See the top three times for completions of every world, and in the favorites list, see your top completion time for each world.
