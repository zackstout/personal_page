Vector Fields

Roman Emperors

  • User can choose a category (e.g. way of rising to power), a particular (e.g. seizing power), and then a slice (e.g. by dynasty or birth province).
  • Results display on a bar chart against a background of *all* emperors sliced in the same way.
  • Built with Flask, Pandas and Chart.js; here lies the repository.

Genetic Algorithm

  • My first serious foray into machine learning. Instead of starting with neural nets, I decided to start with an algorithm all of whose pieces I understood.
  • The goal is to determine which angle is "best" for plants to spawn their leaves at: which angle maximizes surface area of leaves exposed to sunlight?
  • Here's the code, in which I try to follow the principles of object-oriented programming.

Cellular Automata

  • An interface for users to explore the strange patterns that emerge from cellular automata (the 1-dimensional progenitors of the Game of Life).
  • The most fun part of working on this app was trying to find the most elegant way to represent the transformation rules (ultimately, as binary numbers).
  • Built with p5.js and jQuery.
  • Here's the Github repo.
